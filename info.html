click on image to view 		This project was done alongside the video project, and tasked with creating an infographic highlighting a specific technological communication chain.

For my project I decided to do it on morse code and the telegraph, seeing it as a simple enough premise that would give a lot of great information. Due to the issues mentioned in the video project I did end up not having as much time to work on it as I had thought, but in this case I do think I made the most out of what time was given. The most I could say is things are a tad information dense, as well as maybe some retroactive changes to the images used. Overall though I did feel it came out solid enough, especially given the circumstances.
