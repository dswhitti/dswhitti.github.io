Completed Bookmark 		One of the first major projects done in this class, this bookmark was used as an introduction to indesign and production-level printing processes.

The bookmark was designed under the considerations of it being used as a library "reading program" type of bookmark. To avoid waste in printing a mass amount of bookmarks, and so they were made appropriate for an elementary school library and set to ship for the library of Letort Elementary School.

Looking back there were some considerations that were missed when it came to the assignment structure, namely I neglected to link an image to showcase how an understanding of how that then gets processed in printing, but in terms of general design I am quite happy with how the final result turned out.
