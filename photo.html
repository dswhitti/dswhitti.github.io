This project was a combined introduction to photography and photoshop. For it a series of 8 photos were planned out and taken, and from those the best 4 were selected to be brought into photoshop and have edits made to improve their quality for a print format.

To be honest this was my first proper time using photoshop, especially for image touchups. I've done some minor edits with other programs in the past, but most all could be described as cropping jobs or repositioning text on an image.

In terms of the photos themselves I am decently proud of how most came out. I had a lot of fun just wandering around finding good angles to frame images, and there were quite a few whose composition came out really great. I can now see a few edits I would make differently now that I have hindsight going back, but as an overall product I'm glad with what I was able to do with the experience I had.
